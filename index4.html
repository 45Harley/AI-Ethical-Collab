What

This toolkit provides a systematic approach to spiritual discernment based on Christian Science metaphysics. Analyze expressions to distinguish between what truly manifests divine nature and what represents error requiring correction.

All human endeavors can be examined through this lens - art, language, science, healing, governance - determining how they express or obscure divine qualities.

Why

Christian Science teaches that understanding the divine nature and its expressions leads to healing and spiritual progress. By systematically analyzing what truly expresses God versus what represents error, practitioners develop clearer spiritual discernment.

This process supports the fundamental Christian Science practice of replacing false beliefs with spiritual understanding, leading to demonstration of divine power in daily experience.
